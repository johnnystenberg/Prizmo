Common questions

Why is the tax “approximate”?
Local rates and fees can vary by city and item. Prisio uses the best published state/province rate to get you close — treat it as a guide, not a receipt.

Do I need an account?
No. Prisio works entirely on your iPhone with no sign-up.

Is Pro a subscription?
No — Pro is a one-time purchase (~129 kr). Buy it once and it's yours.

Restoring a purchase?
Open Prisio → Settings → Restore purchase, or email us and we'll sort it out.
