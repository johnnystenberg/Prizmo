Privacy Policy

Prisio — Travel Price Scanner · Last updated: 8 June 2026

The short version: Prisio does its work on your device. It has no account, collects no personal data, uses no analytics or tracking, and your scan history never leaves your phone. The only network request fetches public exchange rates and contains none of your personal information.

Who we are

Prisio (“the app”) is provided by the developer reachable at support@prisio.io. For privacy matters this developer is the data controller. [If required in your jurisdiction, add your legal name and address here.]

Information we do not collect

We do not ask you to create an account or sign in. We do not collect your name, email, contacts, photos library, advertising identifiers, or usage analytics. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising SDKs.

What happens on your device

  • Camera. The live camera feed is processed entirely on your device to read the price and currency on a tag using Apple’s on-device text recognition. Camera frames are not photographed, saved, or transmitted anywhere.
  • Scan history. Scans you make are stored locally on your device only. They are not synced to any server and are not accessible to us.

Location

If you grant location permission, your approximate location is used on the device only to suggest the right currency and sales-tax region for the price you’re scanning. Your location is not stored by us and is not transmitted to us or to any third party. You can use the app without location by choosing the region manually, and you can revoke the permission at any time in iOS Settings.

Network requests

To convert prices, the app downloads current foreign-exchange rates from a public rates provider (the Frankfurter API, frankfurter.dev). This request asks only for exchange-rate data and contains no personal information about you. As with any internet request, the provider’s servers may process technical information such as your IP address to deliver the response; please refer to that provider’s terms. We receive only the rate data in return.

Purchases

Prisio Pro is a one-time in-app purchase processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple handles the transaction and any payment details; we never see or store your payment information. Purchase validation is performed using Apple’s StoreKit on your device. Apple’s handling of purchases is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Data retention and deletion

Because your data lives only on your device, you remain in control. Deleting your scan history in the app removes it, and deleting the app removes all of its local data from your device.

Children

Prisio is rated 4+ and does not collect personal data from anyone, including children.

Your rights

Since we do not collect or hold any personal data about you, there is no personal data for us to access, correct, export, or erase on our side. If you have questions about your privacy, contact us using the email below.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the “Last updated” date above.

Contact
