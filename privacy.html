Privacy

Your data stays with you.

Last updated · January 2026

Prisio does its work entirely on your iPhone. There is no account, no server, and no tracking. We don't collect your scans, your location, or anything else about you.

What we collect

Nothing. Prisio has no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no crash trackers that identify you. We have no servers that store your information.

The camera

Prisio uses your camera only while you're scanning a price, and only on your device. Images are processed on-device and are never uploaded or saved unless you explicitly save a scan to your own history.

Exchange rates

To convert currencies, the app periodically downloads public exchange-rate data. These requests fetch rates only — they don't send your scans, prices, or identity. Pro caches rates so you can work offline.

Purchases

Buying Pro is handled by Apple's App Store. Prisio never sees your payment details. Apple's privacy policy governs that transaction.

Your history

If you save scans (Pro), they live in the app on your device. Delete the app or clear your history and they're gone. Nothing syncs to us.

Children

Prisio is suitable for all ages and collects no personal data from anyone.
