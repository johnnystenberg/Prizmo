Getting the best scans

  • Fill the on-screen box with the price — hold steady so it locks on.
  • Good, even light helps; avoid glare and deep shadows on the tag.
  • Aim at one price at a time when tags are close together.
  • The shutter activates once the app has a confident reading.

It read the wrong price — how do I fix it?

Real price tags can be tricky (sale prices, tiny “raised” cents, multi-buy offers). On the result screen you can:

  • Tap another number the app detected on the tag to switch to it.
  • Move the decimal point with the “Wrong decimal?” control (e.g. turn 839 into 8.39) — handy for tags where the cents are printed very small.

About the tax and the converted price

In the US and Canada, sales tax is added at checkout, so Prizmo estimates it by state or province to show a price closer to what you’ll actually pay. In Europe and most other regions prices already include VAT, so no tax is added. Tax estimates and exchange rates are for guidance only and may differ from the final amount you’re charged.

Free vs. Prizmo Pro

You can scan up to 10 prices per month for free. Prizmo Pro is a one-time purchase (no subscription) that unlocks unlimited scans.

I bought Pro but it isn’t showing

If you reinstalled the app or got a new device, open the app’s settings and choose Restore Purchases. Make sure you’re signed in with the same Apple Account you used to buy. If it still doesn’t restore, email us and include the date of purchase.

Requirements
